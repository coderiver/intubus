Company background

In 2012, Vitaly Soldatenko started a company for distribution of digital content on the USA market. Despite the fact that most of the sales come from industry giants such as Amazon, iTunes, Barnes and Noble, and others still at the research stage has been found a new opportunity. The fact is that by 2011-2012 the amount of personal content began to grow leaps and bounds since the tools have changed significantly for its production and to lower the threshold of entering the market. But this created another problem – how to find a customer for the young authors. Traditional market places no longer works for many people. In other words, if you’re not Ernest Hemingway, then you have no one searches on Amazon, for example. Just nobody knows you, so you did not exist for the customers. To increase the number of contacts with potential customers has developed a content-driven platform for digital distribution Intubus. The essence of the solution is this – when you upload a file (music, books, photos, etc., any of digital file) into the Intubus network the Ad-unit with the file appears on the thousands of others relate to your places – blogs, readers, market places, stores, iPods, etc. The system is similar to a social graph, and thus achieved connections between an author and his potential customers. Facebook Store is a good channel to promote and sell your digital goods. Creating your shop you add a new feature, and strengthen the capacity of the distribution network. From this point your content appears on thousands of websites, blogs and widgets of our partner networks and in addition in your Facebook account. The widgets contains of your products and other Intubus member’s products in addition. It works like a Social Graph and has very viral effect. Once you uploaded your file it appear on the thousands of other widgets of our creators and partners.
